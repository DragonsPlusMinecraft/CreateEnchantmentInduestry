## Create: Enchantment Industry 1.2.9

# **IMPORTANT NOTICE**: PLEASE READ BEFORE UPDATE
Due to the update of Create DragonLib, if you are using CCK (Create Central Kitchen), please update to 1.3.11 or above as well.
